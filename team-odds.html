Understanding the Odds
Input Format

Enter American odds (e.g., +500, -110) for each team and round.

Odds Meaning

Each column shows the odds of a team advancing TO that round. For example, "Sweet 16 Odds" represents the probability of a team making it to (reaching) the Sweet 16.

Calculation

The system automatically converts American odds to implied probabilities and adjusts for the vig.
